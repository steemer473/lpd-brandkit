Design Principles Applied

  • Brand Colors: Navy blue (#005792), gradient (cyan to orange), brand consistency
  • Typography: Large, readable headlines (2.2em-2.8em), clear hierarchy
  • Visual Hierarchy: Logo prominence, headline focus, supporting text
  • Readability: High contrast, sufficient spacing, text shadows where needed
  • Brand Voice: Partnership-focused messaging, professional tone, growth-oriented
  • Best Practices: 1200x630px ratio, text within safe area, brand logo visible

How to Generate PNG Files

  1. Browser Screenshot: Open in browser, use DevTools to capture each preview at exact dimensions
  2. Puppeteer/Playwright: Use headless browser to render HTML and capture screenshots
  3. Design Software: Recreate designs in Figma/Photoshop using brand colors and typography
